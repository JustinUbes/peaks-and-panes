Our Story

A Local Contractor You Can Actually Trust

Peaks and Panes was founded on a straightforward belief: homeowners in the Bux-Mont area deserve a contractor who treats their home with the same care and honesty they would expect from a neighbor — because that’s exactly what we are.

Owner Sean Glanzmann grew up in Souderton, Pennsylvania, and has remained deeply connected to the community throughout his career. After years of working in the exterior home improvement industry, Sean recognized a recurring frustration among local homeowners: too many contractors were delivering inconsistent workmanship, unclear pricing, and poor follow-through after the job was done.

He founded Peaks and Panes to be the answer to that problem. Every project — whether it’s a full roof replacement, new siding, or a window and door upgrade — is approached with the same commitment to quality materials, clean execution, and straightforward communication from the first call to the final walk-through.

Serving homeowners across Montgomery and Bucks Counties, Peaks and Panes has built its reputation one project at a time, through referrals and repeat customers who know they can count on us to show up, do the work right, and stand behind it.
